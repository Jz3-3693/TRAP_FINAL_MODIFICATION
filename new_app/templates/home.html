About Us

The Traffic Accident Prediction System is a groundbreaking platform designed to enhance road safety by leveraging historical accident data, live traffic data, and advanced machine learning models. Our mission is to reduce traffic accidents and save lives by providing actionable insights to commuters, city planners, and transportation authorities.

By identifying accident-prone hotspots, predicting accident-prone times, and offering data-driven visualizations, our system empowers users with the tools needed to make informed decisions and foster safer roads for everyone.
